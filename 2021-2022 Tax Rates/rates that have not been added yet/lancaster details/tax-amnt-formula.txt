    school_overall_rate = school_operating + school_debt
    other_overall_rate = USC_lancaster + courthouse_security

####
final_tax_value = (milled_value * county_GRAND_overall_rate) - ( {***ONLY IF residential_multiplier EQUALS 0.04**} milled_value * school_operating) - (purchase_price * ANY "Local Option Sales Tax Credit Factors that apply)
